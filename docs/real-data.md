# Explorable BYO - Real World Data Reference

This document contains the verified, real-world data and classifications for the 13 venues featured in the Explorable BYO application.

## 🏨 Hotels & Lodges

### 1. Holiday Inn Bulawayo
*   **Classification:** Hotel | Luxury | Family Friendly
*   **Price Range:** $130 - $220 / night
*   **Rooms:** 157 rooms (Standard, Suites)
*   **Accessibility:** 4 designated accessible parking spaces with kerb ramps, flat step-free path to entrance. Elevator access to all floors. Ramp access to main property, lounge, and leisure facilities.
*   **Description:** A well-known international brand offering reliable comfort, an outdoor pool, and a restaurant.

### 2. The Bulawayo Club
*   **Classification:** Hotel | Historic | Boutique
*   **Price Range:** $46 - $130 / night
*   **Rooms:** 15 rooms (Standard, Premium, Executive Suites)
*   **Accessibility:** Offers accessible parking, wheelchair-accessible restaurant and lounge area, and an elevator.
*   **Description:** Historic gentlemen’s club offering a step back in time with vintage decor in the city centre.

## 🏡 Guest Houses, Lodges & Villas

### 3. Hillside Manor
*   **Classification:** Guest House | Suburban | Budget Friendly
*   **Price Range:** $33 - $56 / night
*   **Accessibility:** Entire unit located on the ground floor. Highly wheelchair accessible.
*   **Description:** A tranquil suburban getaway offering a comfortable, home-like environment with flat access throughout.

### 4. Bulawayo Central Lodge
*   **Classification:** Lodge | Central | Budget Friendly
*   **Price Range:** $28 - $180 / night
*   **Accessibility:** Wheelchair-accessible parking and a wheelchair-accessible path of travel.

*   **Rooms:** 6 en-suite rooms

## 🌳 Inclusive Visitor Attractions

### 10. Bulawayo Railway Museum
*   **Classification:** Museum | Historic | Educational
*   **Address:** Raylton, Bulawayo
*   **Entry Fee:** $2 Adults / $1 Children
*   **Accessibility:** All areas and surfaces are wheelchair accessible.
*   **Description:** An extensive collection of vintage railway equipment, including steam locomotives and Cecil Rhodes’ personal coach.

### 11. Hillside Dams Conservancy
*   **Classification:** Park | Nature | Active
*   **Entry Fee:** $3 Adults / $2 Children / $1 Vehicle
*   **Activities:** Walking, Ziplining, Canoeing
*   **Accessibility:** Flat outdoor areas, wide pathways, and an on-site wheelchair-accessible restaurant.
*   **Description:** A serene nature reserve offering walking trails and water activities near the city.

### 12. Centenary Park & Natural History Museum
*   **Classification:** Museum | Park | Educational
*   **Entry Fee:** Museum is $3 Adults / $1 Children (Park is Free)
*   **Accessibility:** Level, paved walking trails alongside flat ground-floor access into the museum exhibits.
*   **Description:** The largest museum in Zimbabwe surrounded by a beautiful free public park.

### 13. Silwane Nature Reserve
*   **Classification:** Nature Reserve | Adventure | Family Friendly
*   **Address:** Glenville Drive, Bulawayo
*   **Entry Fee:** $2 Adults / $1 Children
*   **Activities:** Quad Bikes, Boat Rides, Zipline ($3 - $10)
*   **Accessibility:** Operates wheelchair-accessible main entrances and level parking spots.
*   **Description:** An adventure-focused nature reserve offering thrilling outdoor activities just outside the CBD.
